Podcast interview with Jonathan Kahn

Posted: 9th August, 2013

Ahead of speaking at Dare conference this September, I was interviewed by Jonathan Kahn, one of Dare’s organisers, about being honest, client relationships and mentoring in the web industry.

It was lovely chatting to Jonathan, and his podcast also has a proper transcript if you’re more of a reading person than a listening person :)

A bit about me

Hello! I’m a British designer, living in Ireland, and working as one half of Ind.ie. We make an app called Better that protects you while you’re browsing the web. My book Accessibility For Everyone is available in paperback and ebook from A Book Apart.
