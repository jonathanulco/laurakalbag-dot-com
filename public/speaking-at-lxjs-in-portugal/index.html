Speaking at LXJS in Portugal

Posted: 8th June, 2013

I’m really looking forward to October, where I get to return to Portugal to speak at LXJS in Lisbon.

One of the reasons I jumped at the chance to speak at LXJS was their fantastic Code of Conduct. After hearing some horror stories from other women speakers, I wish more conferences had this sort of approach. It’s friendly, not strict, and lets everybody know where they stand.

The tickets are incredibly reasonable for a two-day event, and it looks like Lisbon is well-worth a holiday so a conference there is a bonus!

A bit about me

Hello! I’m a British designer, living in Ireland, and working as one half of Ind.ie. We make an app called Better that protects you while you’re browsing the web. My book Accessibility For Everyone is available in paperback and ebook from A Book Apart.
